What an interview is like

Every brother has a story worth keeping. Here's the arc we follow — from the young man you were to the wisdom you've earned.

  1. Before NIUThe end of high school and the young man you were then.
  2. Finding Delta SigsHow you got to NIU and found the brotherhood.
  3. The Delta Sig yearsAs many stories as you can remember.
  4. Right afterWhat you did immediately after graduating.
  5. Career & familyThe road from then to now.
  6. Where you are todayLife right now.
  7. Current hobbiesWhat you're into these days.
  8. Future plans & goalsWhat's still ahead.
  9. Your familyThe people who matter most.
  10. Advice to your younger selfWhat you'd tell a young man just starting his career and family.

Why we're doing this

Forty years is a long time between beers.

Delta Sigs started at NIU in 1985 — a few guys who decided that if the fraternities on campus weren't worth the price, they'd build their own. They did, and it shaped the friendships of everyone it touched.

Four decades later, we're scattered across the country and out of touch. This project is simple: sit down with the brothers, record their stories, and use them to find our way back to the same table. Listen, reconnect, and add your own.
