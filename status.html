July 19, 2026

Interview #2 — Mike Siegel
  • ✓Interview #2 — "Start From Nothing" (Mike Siegel)The second Founders' Series episode is built. Gary recorded three separate conversations with Mike over the weekend; those were merged into one, run through Studio Sound and filler-word cleanup (smooth, no harsh cuts), and edited into a single continuous interview. Written up as an editorial feature in the same style as Brent's — a full life-story article (south-suburbs kid → founding Delta Sigs from nothing → the Berlin Wall in '89 → the terrifying first open mic → a life on stage) with pull-quotes. Added to the interview grid on the home page as Founders' Series No. 2.
  • ✓Choose your length — full, or a 60 / 30 / 15 / 5-minute cutA brand-new feature. Mike's is a long, wandering two-and-a-half-hour conversation — worth every minute — but not everyone has that kind of time. So the player now lets you pick: the full episode, or a tighter one-hour, thirty-minute, fifteen-minute, or five-minute version. Each shorter cut lives inside the longer one, so you never lose the thread — you just get more of the story as you go up.
  • ✓Chapters on every version, topic "chimes," and a changing photoEvery length is chaptered so you can jump straight to a moment (the full has 18 chapters). Because it's easy to lose track of when the subject changes, a short banjo now bleeds in under the audio at each new topic. And the player photo changes with each chapter — moving from young Mike to present-day Mike as the story travels through his life.
  • ✓Two videos from the tripCut a Day 2 highlight video — paddleboarding out of the marina, then into the ocean — set to music, and paired it with the Day 1 dolphins clip. Both play full-screen. The huge phone originals were compressed to web-friendly versions and served from Cloudinary.
  • ✓Member-editable photo & video galleriesMike's page has two scrolling galleries: his restored photo albums (88 old shots, browsable by era — the beach house, spring break, the mountains, Europe '89), and a "weekend in Redondo" gallery that leads with the two videos and then the trip photos. Tap any photo to view it full-screen. And any logged-in brother can hit Edit on a photo to fix its title or add a description — saved for everyone. Since it's members-only, we trust the brothers to document the photos together.
  • ✓Intro voiceover script + restored photosWrote a warm, personal script for Gary to record introducing the episode and the long weekend. Also cleaned up and AI-upscaled several small, low-resolution photos so they hold up as the chapter portraits and gallery shots.

June 14, 2026

Launch, domain, email & events
  • ✓Member login + secure audio (Supabase)Replaced the placeholder password gate with real per-member accounts. Interview audio lives in a private storage bucket and streams via a short-lived signed URL — only approved members can listen, and the file can't be hot-linked. Tested end-to-end.
  • ✓Custom domain — deltasigsniu.comPointed the Cloudflare-registered domain at Netlify using CNAME flattening on the apex (www redirects to the apex). HTTPS/SSL issued and verified live; existing Cloudflare email routing left untouched.
  • ✓Branded email + one-tap approvalNew-member signups send you a branded notification (via Resend, from gary.ricke@deltasigsniu.com) with the brother's full details and an Approve button — a secure, signed link you can tap from your phone. Approving flips their account to "approved" and auto-emails them a branded "You're in" message with a Listen link. The new brother gets a Delta Sigs-branded confirmation email (not a generic "Supabase Auth" one). A Netlify Forms fallback also records every signup so nothing is missed.
  • ✓Expanded signup formNow captures name, Delta Sig pledge class, year graduated NIU, contact phone, and a split address (street / city / state / ZIP). After submitting, the form is replaced by a large thank-you message instead of leaving the inputs on screen.
  • ✓Upcoming events on the home pageProminent square event cards (most-recent on the left): the DILFs show at Lamplighter's (Aug 22, 2026 — Jim Klimas, Joe Nelson & Matt Denten) and NIU Homecoming vs. Air Force (Oct 10, 2026). A pulsing "Submit an event — we'll email alerts to everyone" button opens a popup form with optional flyer/document upload.
  • ✓Brother-submitted interviews & photosA separate tool (contribute) lets any brother record and upload an interview following the question arc, and upload photos independently — files go to Cloudinary and notify the team. Photos use a full-name naming convention.
  • ✓Member-gated internal pagesStatus and brand-guide pages now require a logged-in member; visitors see a "Members only — you must be a member to view this page" overlay with inline login.
  • ✓Interview structure & the bookHome page now lays out the full interview arc (before NIU → finding Delta Sigs → the years → career & family → today → advice to your younger self) and explains the goal: that final answer feeds a book Gary Ricke is writing, dedicated to young men starting out. Added a 4th "Record a brother yourself" panel alongside "We come to you."
